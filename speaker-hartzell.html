Hartzell Gray

Radio and Podcast Host

Hartzell Gray is Kansas City through and through. You’ve heard him on Alt 96.5. He hosts the award winning, KC Morning Show Podcast. (2x Best Local Podcast ‘20, ‘21) He’s a host for KCUR 89.3, NPR in Kansas City, Sports Radio 810 WHB, and is the Voice of Children’s Mercy Park, The Home of Sporting Kansas City, and The Kansas City Monarchs. He is also Part-Owner and “The Voice” of Journey Pro Professional Wrestling and an Eagle Scout.
